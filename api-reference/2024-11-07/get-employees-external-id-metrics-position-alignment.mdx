---
title: 'Get the position alignment metric'
openapi: 'GET /employees/{external_id}/metrics/position_alignment'
---

Retrieve the position alignment for this Employee. Position alignment is an indication of **the extent to which an Employee fits their assigned position within your company**. It is reported as a number between 0 and 1, with the upper end indicating that an Employee is a good fit for their assigned position,
 while a lower score indicates that the employee may have some missing
skills.
